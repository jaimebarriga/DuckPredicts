THE NON-STORY STORY

DUCKTOPUS is the reincarnated spirit of Paul The Octopus.

Born in the world of FATHERDUCK. DUCKTOPUS, was an average duck. Until he got pulled into a black hole in the ocean 4 years ago, thus, brought to year 2014 on mission to carry on the duties of Paul the Octopus, the animale oracle who rose to worldwise fame for correctly predicting the winner of several World Cup 2010 games.

Mystery remains over why DUCKTOPUS is half-duck, half-octopus and what means for his prophecies.
